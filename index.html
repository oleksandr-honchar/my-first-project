Rent vs. Buy: Data Analysis

This repository holds all the Excel files necessary to accompany my first project: Buy vs Rent Dashboard for Poland ⚖️!

Overview

The Buy vs Rent Dashboard for Poland provides a data-driven analysis of the financial implications of renting versus buying a property. It explores the core problem by evaluating when purchasing or renting is more advantageous based on market conditions. Key findings include cost savings, break-even points, and long-term financial outcomes, offering valuable insights for decision-making. The analysis is supported by reliable data sources, a structured methodology, and interactive visualizations that enhance clarity. Advanced Excel formulas, financial modeling, and visualization techniques ensure an accurate and dynamic comparison, making this tool a valuable resource for prospective homebuyers and renters in Poland.

Business Case

  • 📌 Business Context

    Housing affordability in Poland is a growing concern, with rising property prices and fluctuating mortgage rates creating uncertainty for potential buyers and renters. Short-term economic shifts, such as interest rate adjustments and inflation trends, further complicate decision-making.

  • 🔥 Problem Statement

    Many individuals struggle to determine whether buying or renting is the smarter financial choice in Poland’s volatile market. With mortgage rates changing and rental prices rising, making a data-driven decision is challenging without clear cost comparisons.

  • 📊 Solution

    I have developed an interactive Excel dashboard that:

    • 🔎 Compares the cost of buying vs. renting using key financial indicators
    • 📈 Focuses on short-term trends (Aug 2023 – June 2024) to reflect current market conditions
    • 💡 Analyzes factors like mortgage payments, rental costs, interest rates, and inflation to determine affordability over different time horizons

Insights & Impact

📊 Key Insights

  • 🏠 Renting is Cheaper (20-30%)

    Mortgage payments remain high due to interest rates, making renting more affordable short-term

  • ⏳ Break-Even: 10-15 Years

    Buying only becomes cost-effective after a decade; short-term buyers face higher costs.

  • 📉 Interest Rates Drive Affordability

    1% ratedrop cuts mortgage costs by 8-12%. Timing matters.

  • 📊 Rental Prices Rising Slower

    Renting offers financial flexibility, especially in uncertain markets.

  • 📍 City Matters

    Renting is better in Warsaw/Kraków, but buying makes sense in Łódź/Katowice due to lower price-to-rent ratios

🚀 Impact

  • ✅ Smarter Housing Decisions

    Helps buyers and renters make data-driven choices.

  • ✅ Market Insights for Investors

    Clarifies affordability challenges and investment risks

  • ✅ Risk Mitigation

    Reveals cost-saving strategies like waiting for rate drops

This dashboard provides real-time, actionable insights to navigate Poland’s volatile housing market. 📈🏡
