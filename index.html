Rent vs Buy Dashboard for Poland

This repository holds all the Excel files necessary to accompany my first project: Rent vs Buy Dashboard for Poland ⚖️!

This dashboard for the Polish real estate market is designed to assist investors and others in analyzing apartment prices to make informed decisions between renting and buying.

The data comes from a dataset featuring apartment sales and rental listings from Poland's 15 largest cities, including Warsaw, Lodz, Krakow, Wroclaw, Poznan, Gdansk, Szczecin, Bydgoszcz and Lublin. The data contains detailed information on city, apartment size, number of rooms, price (sale or rent), floor level and proximity to the city center.
